    EPSG:4326 (2D) and EPSG:4979 (3D)  aka WGS84
        Geographic Coordinate System used by Google Maps and D3.js
        http://spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/wgs-84/
        http://spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/4979/
        http://www.opengis.net/def/crs/EPSG/0/4326

    EPSG:4269 aka NAD83i
        http://spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/4269/
